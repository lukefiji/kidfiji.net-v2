About

I grew up learning how to use computers at a very young age by first experimenting with Microsoft Paint. In my teenage years, I discovered that Photoshop was installed on my computer and began to tinker around with it, following online step-by-step tutorials, leading to my passion for art and graphic design. When MySpace came around, I applied my knowledge of Photoshop to customize my profile, learning a bit of HTML and CSS along the way. I soon began to appreciate the intricacies of web design and pursued an informatics degree at UC Irvine, specializing in human-computer interaction. I now enjoy designing websites and graphics, for fun.
